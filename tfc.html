TROUBLE FREE CITY ABOUT:
  • It is an event which provides an opportunity to young civil engineers to prove their potential in planning that would avoid the traffic problems in the city. Participants should plan the traffic facilities like roads, bridges and traffic signals for the given plan. Cost of the facilities will be given. In a given time, participants need to complete the plan and estimate the cost of proposal plan.
    • TIME LIMIT:2 hours.
    REGISTRATION FEE: 50/- per head. (Maximum of 2 members per team) STATEMENT: Participant will be provided with a map of a city suffering with severe traffic problems and basic facilities. Cost of the facilities will be given. RULES AND REGULATIONS:
  • Each team should contain minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 members.
  • Basic facilities should be provided (like petrol pump, hospital etc.).
  • Budget of your plan is very important factor in evaluation.
  • Decision of the jury is final.
    • JUDGING CRITERIA:
  • Effectiveness of the plan.
  • Budget of planning.
  • Transport systems introduced.
  • Location and no of basic facilities.
    • CERTIFICATES:
  • Exciting prizes and Merit certificate for winners.
  • Participation certificate will be given to all the participants.
